Who Are We?

Abhigyaan, founded in 2005, is a student-run organization at BITS Goa with the goal of "Education For All," initiated by Director Prof. T.C. Goel. Prof. Basavadatta Mitra serves as the Faculty-In-Charge. It boasts the largest number of volunteers among all clubs, with five running batches both on and off-campus. These include Project Lamani and Disha for underprivileged children, Evening Batch for non-teaching staff's kids, English Batch for housekeeping staff, and Mess Batch for mess workers. Abhigyaan hosts various events annually, promoting patriotism, education, and community engagement, including Independence Day, Teacher's Day, Sports Day, and Abhigyaan Week activities like finance talks and dengue awareness drives.

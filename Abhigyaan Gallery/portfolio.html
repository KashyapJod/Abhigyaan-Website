Did you know?

  • Abhigyaan, founded in 2005 at BITS Goa by Prof. T.C. Goel, aims for "Education For All" with Prof. Basavadatta Mitra as Faculty-In-Charge.
  • Abhigyaan has the largest volunteer base among BITS Goa clubs, running five educational batches on and off-campus.
  • Abhigyaan has projects include Lamani and Disha for underprivileged children, and specialized batches for non-teaching staff's kids, housekeeping staff, and mess workers.
  • Abhigyaan conducts annual events like Independence Day, Teacher's Day, Sports Day, and Abhigyaan Week promote patriotism, education, and community engagement.
  • Abhigyaan Week features finance talks and dengue awareness drives, enhancing educational and health awareness in the community.
